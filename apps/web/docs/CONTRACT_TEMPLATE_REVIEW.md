```
TEXAS DATA PRIVACY AND SECURITY ACT (TDPSA) COMPLIANCE

(a) Applicability. To the extent [Party] processes "personal data" (as defined
in Texas Business & Commerce Code section 541.001), TDPSA (effective July 1, 2024)
applies.

(b) Processor Obligations:
    (i) Process only per documented instructions;
    (ii) Assist with consumer rights under section 541.051:
        - Right to confirm processing and access
        - Right to correct inaccuracies
        - Right to delete
        - Right to data portability
        - Right to opt out of targeted advertising, sale, profiling
    (iii) Assist with data protection assessments (section 541.105);
    (iv) Delete or return data upon termination;
    (v) Demonstrate compliance upon request.

(c) Subcontractor Flow-Down per section 541.152.

(d) Response within 45 days per section 541.052.
```
